Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our WhatsApp Business chatbot service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our WhatsApp chatbot, we may collect the following information:

  • Phone Number: Your WhatsApp phone number is collected to enable communication through the chatbot service.
  • Messages: All messages you send to our chatbot are stored to provide customer service and process your requests.
  • Customer Information: Information you provide during conversations, including but not limited to:
    • Name and contact details
    • Order information and preferences
    • Product inquiries and purchase history
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with our chatbot, including conversation history and timestamps.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our chatbot customer service
  • Process and fulfill your orders and requests
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send you order confirmations and updates
  • Analyze and improve our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously:

  • Your data is stored securely on our servers
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Access to your personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our chatbot service (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processing)
  • Business Partners: We may share order information with our business partners (e.g., Shopify) to fulfill your orders
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and operational requirements
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using our chatbot service at any time by blocking our WhatsApp Business number

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

6. Third-Party Services

Our chatbot service integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Meta (WhatsApp Business API): We use Meta's WhatsApp Business API to facilitate messaging. Your use of WhatsApp is also subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Shopify: We use Shopify to process and fulfill orders. Order information is shared with Shopify in accordance with their Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI: We may use AI services to power our chatbot responses. Your messages may be processed by these services to generate responses.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
